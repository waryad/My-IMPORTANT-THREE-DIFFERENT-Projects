Instructions on how to play the game

    Three possible outcomes: a tie, a win or a loss. Different numbers assign to rock,paper and scissors. Number 1 is for Rock, Number 2 is for Paper,Number 3 is for Scissors.
    Computer will choose random number of choice and play against the user.
  • Player who decides to play rock (number 1) will beat computer if chosen number 3 Scissors because rock breaks scissors.
  • Player who decides to play paper (number 2) will win if computer chosen number 1 Rock because paper covers rock.
  • Player who decides to play paper (number 3)will lose if computer chooses number 3 Scissors because scissors cuts paper.
  • If both players choose the same number, the game is tie.
